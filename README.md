# chinese-glossary
A structured JSON database of the 12 Prescribed Passages (十二篇範文) for the HKDSE Chinese Language Paper 1

This repo is specifically designed to tackle the "Classical-to-Modern" translation questions (the first 2-3 questions in Section A) by mapping specific keywords from the texts to their expected marking-scheme definitions.
🛠 Why this exists

DSE Paper 1 (Reading) often tests specific "content words" (實詞) and "function words" (虛詞) from the 12 passages. Instead of digging through textbooks, this repo provides that data in a dev-friendly format for such purposes as:
- Building flashcard apps for students
- Learning
- Mock exams
- Larping

## Disclaimer
The details in the repository, including but not limited to the definition, range of words selection, acceptable answers, are not an official publiciation by the HKEAA, and may subject to errors. Please be reminded that Classical Chinese is notoriously fluid. A word like 「而」 or 「以」 can have different interpretations depending on the commentary (e.g., 郭象 vs. 朱熹). We prioritize the definitions most commonly accepted in the DSE context.

## Reporting Errors & Contributing
If you find a typo, a wrong translation, or a word that better fits, open an Pull Request/issue.
